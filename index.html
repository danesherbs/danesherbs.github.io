Background

I am a Machine Learning Scientist at Babylon Health, currently focused on graph convolutions applied to large knowledge graphs. Previously, I was a Research Assistant at Imperial College under William Knottenbelt and a Machine Learning Intern at Samsung. I received my MEng in Mathematics and Computer Science from Imperial College London with First Class Honours, where I worked with Murray Shanahan and Pedro Mediano on disentangling generative factors in visual scenes while preserving spatial information.
