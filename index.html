Modelling natural convection in an annulus

Rayleigh-Bénard convection is a type of natural convection (movement of fluid has no external input) in a horizontal plane that's heated on the bottom and cooled from the top. This work models Rayleigh-Bénard convection in an annulus, which is heated in the center and cooled on the outside.
